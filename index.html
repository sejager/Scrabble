How To

IF POP-UPS ARE DISABLED YOU WILL NOT GET ALERTS ABOUT INVALID SUBMISSIONS!

Drag the tiles from your hand onto the board to create words.

The tiles must start from the first board space and cannot
have any gaps.

Hitting submit will submit that word.

You will be awarded points based on the value of the letter
and any board space bonuses.

You will receive as many tiles to your hand as you have played.

If at any time you feel you cannot make a word, you can select
New Hand to replace all the tiles currently in your hand (those
placed on the board will be unaffected), at the cost of one point per tile.

If you want to quit or you can no longer create any new words
because you ran out of tiles, hitting End Game will submit your
score to the leaderboard.
